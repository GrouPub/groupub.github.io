A Location-based Pub Quiz Application

...natural reason to meet and communicate with others

Users of Pub Quiz will be notified of pop quizzes that will take place in different locations (like pubs or cafes). The purpose of Pub Quiz is to create an environment where users can socialize with ease since there is a clear objective of winning the pop quiz and an opportunity of participating in a group which will create a natural reason to meet and communicate with other users.
